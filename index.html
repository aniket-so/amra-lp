Welcome to Amra Circle

Welcome to the Amra, Early Learning Center, a nurturing and stimulating environment where children embark on an exciting journey of growth, discovery, and development. We are committed to providing exceptional early childhood education that lays a strong foundation for lifelong learning.

OUR PROGRAMMES

Parent - Infant

For children up to 1.5 years

In our Parent-Infant Program, we offer a range of activities and resources to enhance the parent-child connection and promote healthy development. It is a specially curated program designed to support expecting and new parents in their journey of parenthood.

Toddler

For children aged 1 to 3 years

This program is designed to provide a nurturing and stimulating environment where toddlers can thrive and reach important milestones in their physical, cognitive, social, and emotional development.



Preschool

For children aged 3 to 6years

In our child-focused programme, We prioritise the interests, abilities, and well-being of each child. Our program embraces this innate curiosity and creates a supportive environment where children can actively engage in hands-on learning experiences.


Amra Club

For children aged 3 to 12 years

Our after-school program is designed to provide an enriching environment for children to pursue their passions outside of the traditional school setting. From art and dance to music and sports, this programme will boost their confidence, and ignite love for learning.

AMRA’S ENVIRONMENT ENABLES LEARNING THROUGH..

Sensory Play

Sensory activities allow children to refine their thresholds for different sensory information helping their brain to create stronger connections



Role Play

Children use imaginative play to make sense of their ideas and feelings, their identity, their experiences of the world, and their place in it. It helps them regulate their emotions



Nature Play

Natural environments support children’s imaginative play and the development of peer relationships. It helps them make sense of the world, and to learn to care for their environment



Literacy and Numeracy

Children are problem-solvers, pattern-spotters and sense-makers from birth. This curiosity can be nurtured through curated playful opportunities



Art & Music

Children use music, movement and a wide range of materials to express and communicate. Appreciating a child’s multiple perspectives enriches their ways of thinking



Outdoor Play

Time outdoors offers children the chance to challenge their physical self. It also presents the chance to experiment with the larger scales of space, shape and measure
